About

Cake pops are popular around the world for the famous tradition of cake being shaped into a lollipop. The cake pop originated from the idea of the donut hole, which eventually created the cake ball, which turned into the cake pop. Cake pops are very popular and made in many different flavors. Cake pops are made at Starbucks, and are loved by consumers.
