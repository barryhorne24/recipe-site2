Steps

These steps apply to any cake pop flavor.

1. Gather ingredients

- 2 pans

- 3 eggs

- sticks

- Frosting

- Cake mix

- 1/2 cup oil

- 1 cup water

2. create the cake pop!

- Preheat oven to 350 degrees

- combine cake mix, eggs, oil, water, in a bowl

- mix....

- place in the oven for 30-35 mins

- Take the cake out and crumble into a bowl

- Roll the cake into balls

- place in the freezer for 15-20 mins

- Melt frosting in a pan or in the microwave

- Add sticks and sprinkles

3. Enjoy!
